We were fed up with the vast and confusing offer of todays furniture market. So we thought: Why not get back to basics and start all over from there? Honestly, what you really need are a few essentials that are mobile and fit different surroundings. Who knows where you are going to be tomorrow... That's why we have chosen young designs, which are sustainably produced and meet one's daily wear-and-tear requirements. In times of infinite choice and unsustainable consumption, we think true luxury is a few carefully selected objects.
